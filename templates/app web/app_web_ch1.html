Introduction

What is "green economy" for you? What do you know about "green economy"? We begin the chapter discussing this concept and connecting it with the concepts "sustainable energy" and "sustainable development" that will be used during the course
Green Economy

The term ”green economy” is based on multiple conceptual grounds. In fact green economy as a concept has evolved from, and been influenced by, many different schools of economic thought. This section provides a brief overview of how the concept has evolved.
Sustainable Energy

"Sustainable energy" is energy produced and used in such a way that it "meets the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs."
